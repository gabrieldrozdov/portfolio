Sharing Our Stories

for Central Synagogue

Sharing Our Stories is Central Synagogue’s initiative highlighting congregants rather than the synagogue itself. For this project, I developed a visual identity and website that marketed workshops for congregants as well as archived the projects created in such workshops. The brand’s visual identity drew on the themes of conversation, language, and the unique and diverse personalities of Central’s congregation.

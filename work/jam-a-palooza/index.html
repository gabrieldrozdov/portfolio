Jam-A-Palooza

Jam-A-Palooza was a 2+ hour virtual music festival featuring members of the Wesleyan University music and theater communities performing original songs and covers. The event served as a fundraiser for the FGLI fund, which supports students first-generation college students from low-income families. I created the event’s marketing materials, including a playful animated logo.

To Home?? To Home!!

I wrote my first album, To Home, in high school, and it shows. But I really enjoyed writing the album’s instrumentals, and they’re easier to listen to than my voice at times. To Home?? To Home!! features remastered versions of those piano instrumentals.

The album cover is an original acrylic painting originally designed for To Home’s CD package.

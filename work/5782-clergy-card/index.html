5782 Clergy Card

for Central Synagogue

As an annual tradition, Central Synagogue’s lead clergy members send all of their congregants a holiday card commemorating another year of worship. To honor their first year of returning to limited in-person services since COVID, I designed a card that highlighted Central’s connection to NYC’s unique community and culture.

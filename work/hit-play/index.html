Hit Play

for New York Neo-Futurists

The New York Neo-Futurists (NYNF) are known for their unique brand of spontaneous theater, including their flagship act The Infinite Wrench. In place of their live show during COVID-19, the group launched a podcast titled Hit Play. I worked with the group to design the podcast’s logo and artwork, drawing from NYNF’s signature orange and the Infinite Wrench’s hexagonal icon.

Time Passes

Time Passes is a remake of my first album, To Home this time shorter and different. I tried to make it better, but I really mostly made it shorter and different. Ah well.

Like To Home, this album is no longer available to listen to because I wrote it in high school and would prefer not to hear myself sing. If you’d like to hear the piano instrumentals from To Home, please listen to To Home?? To Home!! instead.

The album cover is an original pencil sketch.

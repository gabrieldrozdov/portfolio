Season on Audible Digital Program

for Williamstown Theatre Festival

Due to COVID, Williamstown Theatre Festival was unable to mount their 2020 summer season as planned. Instead of totally abandoning the season, the company partnered with Audible to rework each show as an audio play.

To support this unconventional season, I worked with the theater company on designing and developing a series of show programs in the form of a website. The Festival’s patrons could use the website as a central hub for links to each show, as well as for finding supplementary content like cast bios and artist interviews. For the program’s homepage, I pulled in screen recordings from each production’s Zoom rehearsals.

Four Years Later

for Payton Millet

Four Years Later was an original musical with a book written by Ian Baxley and music composed by Payton Millet. The show was produced by A Class Act, NYU during November 2018 in a production directed by Korey Caron.

For the poster, I created an abstract vector illustration that referenced the show’s setting in a coffee shop and themes of conflict after a messy break-up.

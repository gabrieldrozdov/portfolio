“This is not a show”

The final project for Nancy Skolos’ Graduate Type Studio I at RISD was to create a zine that documented our work during the semester and connected it to a cohesive theme. For my zine, I decided to typset Ashley Lee’s article “Inside the battle to change a prestigious theater festival’s ‘broken’ culture” for the Los Angeles Times. The article discusses the challenges faced by Williamstown Theatre Festival under the leadership of former artistic director Mandy Greenfield.

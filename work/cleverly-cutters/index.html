Cleverly Cutters

for CleverlyCo.

After a year in business, the independent polymer clay jewlery brand CleverlyCo. began creating products for other artists. I designed the branding and technology for the brand’s spin-off Cleverly Cutters, which sold 3D printed clay cutters that let artists create specifically-shaped earrings.

To generate the cutters, I developed an algorithm using Rhinoceros 3D and Grasshopper that would turn vector illustrations into 3D models. I then printed these models myself for distribution.

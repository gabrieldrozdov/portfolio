Motion Experiments

While isolated in my parent’s basement at the start of a global pandemic, I thought it could be fun to get better at animation. So, I worked on several short projects as exercises in motion design.

These projects span two main categories: vector illustration, which consists of abstract graphics and drawings; and, kinetic typography, which is all about type.

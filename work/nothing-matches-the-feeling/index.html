“Nothing matches the feeling of finding treasure”

During RISD’s 2022 winter session, I took Mark Cetilia’s course Of Sound and Vision in which I created several generative art programs (i.e. patches) using Max/MSP/Jitter. “Nothing matches the feeling of finding treasure” explores a lo-fi, bowling alley-inspired, WordArt-esque aesthetic that makes use of Jitter’s limited typography features to generate a video from a text document.

Scent Discovery

at Local Projects, for the World of Coca-Cola

The Scent Discovery exhibit at World of Coca-Cola is an immersive experience that focuses on the sense of smell. Entering the exhibit, visitors are greeted with an interactive wall containing a variety of distinct smells. This section leads to a guided game in which visitors have to guess the correct scent out of a series of options.

While interning in the Visual Experience Design department at Local Projects, I helped with the project’s concept and design development phases. Working with my supervisor Olivia Crosby, I created mockups and renderings for several design directions, participated in team and client meetings, and helped push the project toward completion. The project was completed shortly after my internship was over and features iterations of my work that were refined into final designs.
